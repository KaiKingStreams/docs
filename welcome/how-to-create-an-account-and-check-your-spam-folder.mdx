## Part 1: Creating an Account

### Step 1: Visit the Website

Open your browser and go to our official website.

This can be one of the following websites

- [Kai's Place](https://kaikingstreams.xyz)
- [Darkmoon Hollow](https://darkmoonhollow.xyz)
- [Darkmoon Chronicles](https://drakmoonchronicles.com/)

### Step 2: Click “Sign Up” or “Create Account”

Locate the **Sign Up**, **Register**, or **Create Account** button.\
This is usually found at the top right of the page or on the homepage.

### Step 3: Fill Out the Registration Form

Enter the required information:

- Nickname
- Username
- Email address (make sure it’s spelled correctly)
- Password (follow any listed requirements)

Once finished, submit the form.

### Step 4: Check for the Verification Email

After submitting the form, you should receive a verification email within a few minutes.\
This email is required to activate your account.

---

## Part 2: Checking Your Spam or Junk Folder

Sometimes verification emails are filtered automatically. If you don’t see the email in your inbox, follow these steps.

### Step 1: Open Your Email Provider

Log in to the email account you used during registration.

### Step 2: Go to the Spam or Junk Folder

Look for folders labeled:

- **Spam**
- **Junk**
- **Promotions** (for Gmail users)

### Step 3: Find the Verification Email

Search for an email from our site.\
If found, open it.

### Step 4: Mark the Email as “Not Spam”

This helps ensure future emails arrive in your inbox.

- Click **Not Spam** or **Move to Inbox**

### Step 5: Click the Verification Link

Inside the email, click the verification or activation link to finish setting up your account.

---

## Part 3: Still Not Receiving the Email?

If you still don’t see the email:

- Double-check that you entered the correct email address
- Wait 5–10 minutes and refresh your inbox
- Try resending the verification email if the option is available
- Contact support if the issue continues
