## Executive Leadership

### President

Holds the highest level of authority within the Server and Company. The President oversees all major decisions, long-term vision, and overall direction. This role ensures that leadership, development, and community goals remain aligned.

### Vice President

Acts as second-in-command and assists the President in overseeing operations. The Vice President steps in when the President is unavailable and helps coordinate leadership efforts across all departments and staff teams.

### Chief of Staff

Serves as the primary coordinator between executive leadership and staff. The Chief of Staff manages internal communication, assists with planning and execution, and ensures leadership decisions are properly implemented.

## **_Internal Affairs Division (Reports to Chief of Staff)_**

### Director of Internal Affairs

Leads the Internal Affairs Division and is responsible for oversight of staff conduct, investigations, and internal accountability. This role ensures fairness, professionalism, and integrity across all departments while remaining neutral and unbiased.

### Deputy Director of Internal Affairs

Assists the Director and assumes leadership duties when necessary. The Deputy Director helps manage investigations, review reports, and coordinate with executive leadership on sensitive matters.

### Internal Affairs Supervisor

Oversees Internal Affairs Officers and manages active cases. This role ensures investigations are handled properly, documentation is accurate, and procedures are followed consistently.

### Internal Affairs Officer

Conducts investigations involving staff behavior, abuse of power, policy violations, or internal disputes. Officers collect evidence, interview involved parties, and submit detailed reports to supervisors.

### Internal Affairs Analyst

Focuses on reviewing logs, reports, patterns, and data related to staff conduct. Analysts assist investigations by identifying trends, compiling evidence, and maintaining records.

### Internal Affairs Trainee

Entry-level members of the Internal Affairs Division learning investigation procedures, documentation standards, and confidentiality practices under supervision.

## **Administration & Staff Leadership**

### Head Admin

Oversees the administration team and enforces policies and procedures. This role ensures consistency in moderation, staff conduct, and day-to-day server operations while translating leadership direction into action.

### Admin

Responsible for server moderation, rule enforcement, and administrative tasks. Admins maintain a safe, fair, and organized environment while supporting players and staff when issues arise.

### Senior Staff

Experienced staff members who mentor and guide lower staff ranks. Senior Staff assist with moderation, staff training, and maintaining stability across the team.

### Staff

Core team members who assist the community, moderate chats and gameplay, and respond to reports and concerns. Staff are essential to maintaining a positive and welcoming environment.

### Staff in Training

Entry-level staff members learning rules, procedures, and responsibilities under supervision. This role prepares new staff for full Staff positions.

### MVP+ (Byable Tier)

The highest player rank, offering the most perks available to non-staff members. MVP+ players receive premium quality-of-life features, cosmetic bonuses, and top-tier recognition for supporting the server.

### MVP (Byable Tier)

An advanced supporter rank with expanded perks and enhanced gameplay features. MVP players receive greater access to convenience features and increased visibility within the community.

### VIP+ (Byable Tier)

An upgraded supporter rank providing additional quality-of-life improvements and cosmetic enhancements beyond VIP.

### VIP (Byable Tier)

A supporter rank that grants small perks and server recognition. VIP players help support ongoing development and server costs.

### Dwellers

The default starting rank for all players. Members have access to standard survival gameplay and core server features.

### Unconfirmed Member

 A user account that has been created but not yet fully verified. While in this state, access to certain features or areas of the community may be restricted until the required confirmation steps are completed.
