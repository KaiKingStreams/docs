## Creating your account

To get started, you need to create an account:

1. Visit the registration page
2. Enter your email address and choose a secure password
3. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox
4. Complete your profile information

## Account configuration

### Profile settings

Update your profile information:

- **Username**: Choose a unique username for your account
- **Display name**: Set how your name appears to others
- **Email**: Update your contact email address
- **Password**: Change your password from the security settings

### Security settings

Protect your account with these security features:

- **Two-factor authentication**: Enable 2FA for additional security
- **Login alerts**: Get notified of new login attempts
- **Active sessions**: Review and manage active sessions

### Preferences

Customize your account preferences:

- **Notifications**: Choose which notifications you want to receive
- **Privacy**: Control who can see your information
- **Language**: Select your preferred language
- **Theme**: Choose between light and dark mode

## Troubleshooting

### Can't access your account?

If you're having trouble logging in:

1. Use the "Forgot password" link to reset your password
2. Check your spam folder for verification emails
3. Contact support if you still can't access your account

### Email verification issues

If you didn't receive a verification email:

1. Check your spam or junk folder
2. Request a new verification email from your account settings
3. Ensure you entered the correct email address
